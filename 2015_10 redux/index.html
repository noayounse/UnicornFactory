Noa Younse is a designer located in the Jersey City. He earned his Bachelor's Degree in Architecture from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo, and his Master of Science in Advanced Architectural Design from Columbia, and has since taken an interest in utilizing that design background in conjunction with computational techniques to visualize data and generate algorithmic art. He has worked on various full scale digital installations and participated in several data workshops with an emphasis on using Processing to analyze and interpret datasets.

Currently he is trying to change the world with a little code and a lot of hard work at The Office for Creative Research.
